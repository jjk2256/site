EchoPulse (2025)

EchoPulse is an interactive game that visualizes players' real-time heart rates through dynamic, pulsating waves. Created in collaboration with Skylar Zhang, the project uses biofeedback technology and physical computation to transform biometric data into a responsive visual interface.

By integrating heart rate sensors, the game enables participants to observe and influence their physiological signals, fostering a unique interplay between body and environment.
